About TimberConnex

TimberConnex is Australia’s independent platform for navigating the mass timber supply chain. We help developers, architects, and builders understand feasibility, identify the right suppliers, and reduce risk across design, procurement, and delivery.

What We Do

Mass timber projects require early clarity around cost, logistics, engineering pathways, and supplier capability. TimberConnex provides structured guidance and market intelligence so project teams can make confident decisions.

  • Feasibility Studies – Early-stage viability, cost ranges, risk identification, and carbon benefits.
  • Supplier Shortlisting – Matching your project with manufacturers based on capability, capacity, and lead times.
  • Procurement Advisory – Support through design development, tendering, and contract negotiation.
  • Mass Timber Roadmaps – Clear sequencing and milestones from concept to delivery.
  • Market Insights – Ongoing updates on pricing trends, supply constraints, and policy changes.

Our Approach

We focus on clarity, transparency, and risk reduction. Our feasibility studies and advisory services are designed to give you a realistic understanding of what’s possible, what it will cost, and how to move forward with confidence.
